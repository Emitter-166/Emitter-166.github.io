Eisenhower's task prioritization method calculator

The Eisenhower Method, also known as the Eisenhower Matrix, is a time management tool used to prioritize tasks based on urgency and importance. It divides tasks into four categories:

  1. Urgent and important (do first)
  2. Important, but not urgent (schedule for later)
  3. Urgent, but not important (delegate or outsource)
  4. Neither urgent nor important (eliminate)
